Chapter 1: The Past – Seeds of Change

In 1988, NASA scientist Dr. James Hansen gave a testimony before the U.S. Congress, warning about the dangers of climate change. His testimony is considered one of the key moments in the public’s awareness of climate change. Since then, scientific advancements have continued to shed light on the dire need to take action to protect our planet.

The seeds of climate change awareness were planted decades ago, but efforts to combat it have only intensified in recent years.
